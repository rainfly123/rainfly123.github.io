Golden Sail SARS-CoV-2 Nucleic Acid Test is a real-time RT-PCR test intended for the qualitative detection of nucleic acids from SARS-CoV-2 in nasopharyngeal and oropharyngeal swab samples from patients with possible SARS-CoV-2 infection. The test is clinically validated and verified in accordance with FDA guideline and Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments of 1988 (CLIA) requirements and is performed in PacGenomicsḂŸ clinical laboratory certified under the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments of 1988 (CLIA), 42 U.S.C 263a, to perform high complexity tests, by qualified clinical laboratory personnel. PacGenomics SARS-CoV-2 Antibody Test is a lateral flow immunoassay intended for the qualitative detection and differentiation of IgM and IgG antibodies to SARS-CoV2 in human venous whole blood, plasma from anticoagulated blood, or serum. The test is intended for use as an aid in identifying individuals with an adaptive immune response to SARS-CoV-2, indicating recent or prior infection. The test should not be used to diagnose acute SARS-CoV-2 infection. The test is limited to laboratories certified under the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments of 1988 (CLIA), 42 U.S.C 263a, to perform high complexity tests, by qualified clinical laboratory personnel.






Collection of a nasopharyngeal or oropharyngeal swab specimen is required for nucleic acid testing. Collection of venous whole blood, plasma, or serum is required for antibody testing.
